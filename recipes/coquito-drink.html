Coquito Cocktail

Description

If you enjoyed the coquito recipe, this is the secret to make you the talk of the next holiday party. By thinning out the main blend with extra rum, and then cutting it again with more rum in the glass, the coquito is changed from a slow sipping dessert drink, to a full on bar worth cocktail.

The cinammon syrup is the most important part of this recipe, and an added benfit is you can use it with rum and tequila to make many great cocktails. Feliz Navidad!

Ingredients

  • 3 oz prepared Coquito (recipe
  • 1 teaspoon of prepared cinammon syrup (recipe
  • 1 oz premium Rum*
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Fresh nutmeg for grating

Steps

  1. Add cinnamon syrup, rum, coquito, and ice to shaker
  2. Stir with barspoon
  3. Strain into coupe glass
  4. Top with ground cinnamon and fresh grated nutmeg
  5. Garnish with cinnmon stick
Notes

*Use same rum as Coquito, a non-spiced rum, preferably Puerto Rican.
